Join Re  ∕∕ wire

We are looking for people who want to make research happen, each in their own way.

Re  ∕∕ wire runs on goodwill. There is no entry fee, no formal application process, and no hierarchy. What we ask is simple: be a regular contributor, be transparent about the time you can give, and make sure what you commit to is something you can actually deliver.

How you can be part of it

Researchers with ideas

You work in a lab or in industry. You have research ideas but not enough time or guidance to pursue them on your own. You are looking for collaborators who will discuss your ideas seriously, review your drafts, and push you to get it right.

Get in touch →

Conference representatives

You attend conferences regularly. You would be willing to present a paper on behalf of a Re  ∕∕ wire member who cannot travel. This is one of the most concrete ways to help independent researchers get their work out there.

Get in touch →

Independent contributors

You are a researcher, research engineer, or student. You have spare time and you want to spend it doing real research. You are looking for a structure that gives you collaborators, feedback, and an affiliation for your publications.

Get in touch →

Resource partners

You are a company or an individual with compute resources, GPUs, or infrastructure you are willing to share. Enabling research is often a matter of access to the right hardware at the right time.

Get in touch →

Event & community partners

You organize conferences, workshops, or meetups. You are interested in the Re  ∕∕ wire initiative and willing to support it: special conditions for members, speaking slots, visibility, or simply connecting communities.
