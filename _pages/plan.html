World Domination Plan

How we plan to take over the research world, one milestone at a time. Most of these will not happen in order. None of them are evil.

First meetup

A regular gathering, in person or remote, where members and guests share work and ideas.

First funding

First conference presentation

A Re  ∕∕ wire paper presented at a venue, in person or by a member representative.

Critical mass
